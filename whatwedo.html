We Host Regular Artisan Wine Tastings

Favourite Grapes is a neighbourhood wine bar with a focus on ‘new wave’ wines made by artisan winemakers from all over the world.
We serve honest wines made from sustainably farmed vineyards and with minimal intervention in the cellar.
The bar is the place to discover these quality, lesser-known wines served by a knowledgeable team in an unpretentious, hospitable space.
Oh, and with a killer playlist of course.
Our list changes regularly to ensure you can enjoy something new each time you visit
